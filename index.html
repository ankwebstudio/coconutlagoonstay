About Coconut Lagoon Stay

Experience Luxury, Nature & Authentic Kerala Hospitality

Nestled amidst Kerala’s breathtaking backwaters and lush coconut groves, KeraVista Resort offers a peaceful escape designed for relaxation, wellness, and unforgettable experiences.

From luxury lake-view rooms and traditional Kerala cuisine to rejuvenating Ayurvedic spa therapies, our resort blends modern comfort with Kerala’s rich cultural heritage. Whether you're planning a romantic getaway, family vacation, or wellness retreat, KeraVista promises serenity, elegance, and warm hospitality.
